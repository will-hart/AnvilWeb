The Anvil Mission Generator (AnvilMG) is an open source Windows app that lets you build the skeleton of a compelling Arma2 or ArmA3 mission. It takes the grunt work out of producing a mission, removing the need to micro-manage the placement and armament of units and groups. To see it in action, have a look at some screenshots, or get started below.
