The Anvil Mission Generator (AnvilMG) is a simple Windows utility that lets you build the skeleton of a compelling Arma2 or ArmA3 mission. It takes the grunt work out of producing a mission, removing the need to micro-manage the placement and armament of units and groups.

In particular, Anvil MG lets you easily reuse custom groups or units and allows you to set your unit defaults the way you want to. Want all your groups to be created as playable? No problems! Want your riflemen to be armed with a custom loadout? No problems!

Best of all ArmaMG is free and open source. It is distributed under an MIT license so you are free to contribute or follow the development on github. If you want to see it in action, have a look at some screenshots.
